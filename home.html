What is Box Tower?

Box Tower is a digital community project and gathering space. Contribute a personalized box to the box tower and help build it higher. You'll be able to see where your box is placed as well as who you're box neighbors are. Solidify yourself as a piece of digital history in this work of art and community.

Tell me about my Box?

Your box contributes to this site in more ways than one, and can also be for your own gain. With the ability to personalize your box you can advertise links to your social media or simply give a personal shoutout to whoever you please. On top of that, each box helps fund the longevity of this site and it's maintenance.

How do I keep up with the Box Tower?

Part of Box Tower's fun is monitoring it's growth. Click the 'Update-List' link to be directed to an email sign up form where you can recieve regular updates of the Tower's height and analytics of how many people have viewed you Box.
